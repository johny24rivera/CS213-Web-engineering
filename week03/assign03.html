About Me

I am a musician by heart. Or if you want to get really technical, I'm a guitarist/bassist/drummer/vocalist/songwriter. Everything I've ever written is in essence hope.When I talk about a bad moment in my life, the atrocities of war, the pain of loneliness, when the melody is amplifying the negative emotion, you will always be able to find hope in my music. My songs happen by accident but are full of deep emotion, with lyrics carefully crafted to my perfection, to my vision.The sensation I feel when I perform in front of hundreds of people live just further increase the satisfaction in my heart. I'm being heard.

" A world so cold/ Is all I’ve known/ A world with fear/ Is all they’ve shown. I’ll no longer, Follow your footsteps. From now on, I’m the master of my life. For the first time, I won’t obey. For the first time, I will betray! For the first time, I broke out of your chain. But sadly I’m living in a, World so cold."

While I wrote these lyrics, images of my uncle rushed through my mind. The emotion I braced forth, behind his hammering punches when I was younger resonated within every syllable of my lyric. The anger, the sadness, and the hope comes altogether to conclude this song. Then, it goes to the world as my cry for arms to stand up for ourselves. I don't care if I never get money out of this. I just want to be heard, I want to connect. I want to show the world, I am me.
For that reason, I believe that my pen truly is my greatest weapon.
